Swarm Resolution-Knob Simulator

fixed 3DGS budget · B-spline-coupled surface · infinite-resolution zoom Knot-a-Surface
Pipeline: warm-up subdivides the parameter domain onto true scene geometry; the resolution knob converges all frustums on one ROI and triggers localized fine-tuning at the same fixed Gaussian budget (up to 32,768 splats carrying material texture). The urban map centers on a forward operating base — walls, HQ, tank, APCs, and a moving patrol that the model must keep re-fitting (watch it in ▦ residual view). Commander view renders the reconstruction only: LINK/FREE tethers or detaches it from the swarm, WASD (Shift = sprint) moves the swarm — or flies the commander when detached — R/F works the knob, 📏 measures distance + line-of-sight over the model, ◎ drops grid-referenced markers, ⚡ FORCE OPT bursts optimization, ⇆ swaps views.
